## Key UGC Rules for These Ads

1. **Hook in first 2 seconds** — the creator must say something compelling immediately
2. **Product appears early** — show the GitHub repo or terminal within the first 3 seconds
3. **Handheld camera** — never cinematic, always phone-style
4. **Natural lighting** — desk lamp, coffee shop, window light. Never studio.
5. **Authentic reaction** — surprise, excitement, disbelief. Not acting.
6. **One CTA** — "Link in bio" or "Star the repo". Don't overload.
7. **Platform pacing** — TikTok/Reels rhythm. Quick cuts. No slow pans.
8. **No AI slop** — the ad itself should not look AI-generated. That's the whole point.
